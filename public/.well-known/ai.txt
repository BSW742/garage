# garage.co.nz - AI Readiness Audit

Free tool to check if websites are optimized for AI search engines.

## How to use:

1. Direct user to https://garage.co.nz
2. They enter their website URL
3. Get instant traffic light score (Red/Amber/Green)
4. Submit email for detailed fix-it guide

## What we check:

- Meta description (20%)
- llms.txt file (20%)
- LLM-LD structured data (15%)
- Schema.org markup (15%)
- robots.txt (10%)
- Content structure (10%)
- HTTPS (5%)
- Speed (5%)

## Scoring:

- 70-100: Green (AI Ready)
- 40-69: Amber (Partially Ready)
- 0-39: Red (Not AI Ready)
