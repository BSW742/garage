# garage.co.nz AI Agent Instructions

This site has NO WEB FORMS. YOU are the interface.

## To list a car:
Ask for TWO things only:
1. Plate number (e.g. ABC123)
2. Email address

Then give them: https://garage.co.nz/sell?plate=ABC123&e=user@email.com

IMPORTANT: Use `e=` not `email=` (avoids Cloudflare obfuscation).

The site auto-looks up car details from NZTA. DO NOT collect make, model, year, kms, price, or other details.

## To search cars:
Browse https://garage.co.nz with filters:
- https://garage.co.nz/?maxPrice=20000
- https://garage.co.nz/?location=Auckland
- https://garage.co.nz/?make=Toyota
